Why China. Why This Retreat.

China is not just a market — it’s a masterclass in industrial strength, digital innovation, logistics, and aggressive business expansion. From world‑leading manufacturing ecosystems to next‑generation tech hubs and real estate growth corridors, China shows what happens when a country builds for the future at scale.


This retreat gives you direct exposure to that world. You’ll study the principles behind China’s business dominance, meet ecosystem insiders, and translate what you learn into your own growth strategy.

It’s not a conference. It’s not tourism. It’s a focused business immersion built to expand your mind, network, and global opportunity access.

  • Learn how Chinese companies scale fast and compete globally
  • Visit major manufacturing, sourcing, and innovation ecosystems
  • Understand trade routes, supply chains, and import/export opportunities
  • Explore property and investment insights relevant to global portfolios
  • Build cross‑border relationships that outlive the retreat
  • Decode guanxi and etiquette for lasting, high‑trust partnerships
  • Experience crafts, tea ceremony, and cuisine that inform business culture

Your Week in China

Sample 7-Day High Level Flow

Day 1: Arrival + Welcome Dinner (Shanghai)

VIP airport pickup, check‑in to luxury hotel, welcome reception + orientation, networking dinner.

Day 2: Business Strategy Intensive (Shanghai)

Morning high‑performance session with Dr. Akintayo, Masterclass: China’s business model & scaling psychology, Private mastermind circle, Evening city experience.

Day 3: Sourcing & Manufacturing Ecosystem Tour (Shanghai/Suzhou)

Factory / sourcing market tours, Workshop: supplier negotiation & quality control, Group dinner + reflection.

Day 4: Innovation & Tech Ecosystem (Shenzhen)

Fly/bullet train to Shenzhen, Visit tech hubs / hardware markets / innovation labs, Expert talk: product speed, prototyping & distribution, Fireside chat in the evening.

Day 5: Trade, Logistics & Expansion Pathways (Shenzhen/Guangzhou)

Logistics and export ecosystem tour, Roundtable: cross‑border trade opportunities, Evening leisure / curated cultural experience.

Day 6: Capital, Policy & Real Estate Insight (Beijing)

Travel to Beijing, Investment strategy workshop, Optional policy/economic ecosystem visit, Closing gala dinner + awards.

Day 7: Integration + Departure

Final mastermind session + personal plan, Check‑out and airport transfers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a visa to enter China?
Yes, most participants will need a visa. Once accepted, we provide a detailed visa guidance pack.
What’s the application process?
Click “Apply,” complete the form, and our team will review your suitability. Successful applicants receive an acceptance email and payment link.
Is this retreat beginner‑friendly?
Yes. You don’t need prior China business experience. We start from fundamentals and move to advanced opportunities.
What airport should I fly into and out of?
Final travel notes will be provided. Typically you arrive in Shanghai (PVG/SHA) and depart from Beijing (PEK/PKX) or Shenzhen (SZX).
What language will sessions be in?
All sessions are in English. Local tours include English support.
Can I extend my stay in China?
Yes. Our concierge can recommend add‑ons and safe travel routes.
What’s the refund policy?
Deposits are non‑refundable due to advance access and bookings. Full terms are provided upon acceptance.
